What is Minfulness?

The feeling of stress and overwhelm that comes from responsibilities, deadlines, and problems are part of our lives, and it is not something we can skip easily. Luckily, there is something we can do to improve our mood and recharge ourselves to continue and push through. We need to understand that we have the power to control how we feel or how external stimuli can influence us. Our mind plays a big role in everything we do, and like our body, we can train it to our benefit. Practicing mindfulness through living the present helps us to leave the stress behind and enjoy the moment. According to Merrian-Webster Dictionary, Mindfulness is the practice of maintaining a nonjudgmental state of heightened or complete awareness of oneâ€™s thoughts, emotions, or experiences on a moment to moment basis. In other words, is focusing our attention on the present, like reading a book or eating our favorite food. Also, mindfulness facilitates to savor the pleasures of the present, fully engaged in activities we are doing, and deal with adversities. It benefits our mental and physical health. Invest time in yourself. You are important too.
