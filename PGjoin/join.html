Join the Chamber of Commerce

We invite you to know the membership levels and see which one is the best for you. There are four options. Each one of them has its respective value and benefits. If you have any questions or concerns, do not doubt to contact us.

Membership Application
Level of Membership

Gold

  • 2 Seats at Mayor's Executive Council
  • Banner Advertising at monthly luncheons and chamber events
  • Article in the Monthly Chamber Business Insider Publication
  • Website banner advertisement on Chamber home page
  • Free website promotion, marketplace & business search powered by Chamber Nation
  • 4 Teams at Golf Tournament
  • All benefits of Silver Membership

Silver

  • Two (2) Tickets to exclusive annual business awards gala
  • Free website promotion, marketplace & business search powered by Chamber Nation
  • Corporate Golf Membership Access - 6 times - 9 holes with cart for 4 people / yr. (Fox Hollow Golf Club)
  • Digital Promotion at Monthly Luncheons and Chamber Events
  • All benefits of Bronze Membership

Bronze

  • One seat at monthly business training luncheon - $200 value
  • Free website promotion, marketplace & business search powered by Chamber Nation
  • Corporate Golf Membership Access - 1 time - 9 holes w/cart for 2 people
  • Access to monthly business training luncheon and networking events

Non-profit

  • Access to monthly business training luncheon and networking events
  • Welcome Bag advertising & other marketing tools
  • Free website promotion, marketplace & business search powered by Chamber Nation
