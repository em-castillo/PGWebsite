Site Purpose

The objective of Solstice Season is to accurately communicate to the community about weather story of three towns located in Idaho. These three towns are Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Heaven. Along with providing the weather condition of the places named, the site will provide relevant information about each town. Also, the weather information will be presented in images for the best comprehension.
