Target Audience

The site is directed to a mature-adult audience, which will be over 18 years old, had finished high school, and is looking to spend a few days in Preston, Soda Springs, and/or Fish Heaven. The site welcomes singles, married, parents, grandparents, whoever decides to plan ahead and check if the weather comes along with their plans.


Personas

• Chloe is a 20 years old student from BYU-I who drives back home every weekend to see her family and spend time with them.

• John is a 31 years old father of one baby who goes out every day to work. His work is a couple of blocks away from home, so he has decided to walk there every morning to exercise.


Scenarios

• Demi is a mom of three children. Along with her family, she is going to visit her parents in Preston and is going to stay there for a week. Demi lives in Denver, Colorado. She needs to decide what type of clothes to pack for her family.

• Matt is a young adult of 24 years old. He loves doing sports and enjoys a good weekend in nature. Matt is planning to go to Fish Heaven. He has heard about Bear Lake, but he wants to know other activities he can do according to the weather.

• Simon is a veteran who loves to run every morning at 6 am around the neighborhood. He is always checking the city news to see if there are construction around that he will need to avoid. Also, Simon thinks that he could find information and weather updates to know what to expect during his run.

• Emma and Chris are a married couple whose anniversary is coming in two weeks. They don't want to go too far from their town, so they have decided to spend a few days in Soda Springs. Chris went there once, but he was a boy, so he doesn't remember much about the attractions, weather, or where the Geyser Park was.

• Nicole is a senior college student who is driving back home. How it gets dark pretty soon, she has planned to stay the night in Preston. The next, morning she curious to know if there is something fun in the town and if it's better to walk around or drive the car to get to a fun place.
