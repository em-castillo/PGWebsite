Design Principles Document

Fitt's Law

Swiss Miss

swissmiss.com

Swiss Miss has two principal features on its website: images and a button on them. The centered button in the image brings attention to it, and at the same time, does not overlap the referenced image. Also, it is in an easy to tap location. In this example, the bottom button is bigger than the top one because of its use frequency.

Contrast

National Geographic

nationalgeographic.com

The National Geographic site is recognizable due to its particular yellow color. The website displays three colors: yellow, black, and white. Using a primary color in comparison with monochromatic makes a high contrast and is appealing to the user. Also, there is a contrast in the size and weight of the fonts.

Repetition

One Loved Babe

onelovedbabe.com

Repetition is a design principle that provides consistency to a website, giving a sense of wholeness and relation. The repetitive elements in this clothing boutique website are color and fonts. The mauve color is present in the header, in the background of the image, and the "Message Us" button. The serif, sans serif, and handwriting fonts are shown at least twice.
