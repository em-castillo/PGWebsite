Do You Meditate?

Meditation is the practice of intentionally turning your attention away from distractions and focus on a single point of reference like the breath, body, specific thought, etc. But, How can you do it? First, find a quiet place where you cannot be disturbed, and there are no distractions. Second, sit in a chair or couch with your feet on the ground or lay down if it is more comfortable for you, but be careful to not fall asleep. And finally, close your eyes and start meditating. Some people meditate through yoga, but if you are new to this, try a guided meditation. You can find some on Youtube or Spotify, those are a good place to start. You can meditate as long as it is good for you. You can begin with five or ten minutes, and then increase it as weeks pass by. You will see how much calmer and focus you are. Researches show that meditation has a variety of positive benefits. This includes a positive mood, better concentration, decreases stress, and it can even boost the gray matter. As you see, adding this activity to your daily routine has many advantages.
